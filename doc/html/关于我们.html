ABOUT US

Guangdong Anda Rocky Int. Freight Service Ltd. (Anda Rocky), as a member of IFFA (International Freight Forwarders Association), has been focusing on Africa LCL (less than container load) for over 11 years.

In 2005, Anda Rocky was established. With a vision of being the most trusted company by providing best freight solutions nationally and internationally, we have been persisting on offering China-to-Africa forwarding transportation services which are fast, reliable, affordable and convenient so as to satisfy unique needs of individual and corporation clients.

In 2009, we started door-to-door service in Africa. Upholding a faith of mutual benefit, we have been adhering to provide specially customized purchasing assistance in China for African clients with business invitation letter, hotel reservation, free airport pick-up, guiding translation, forwarding transportation (LCL) and local customs clearance, in the aim of making purchase in China just easy and convenient.

From 2016, we developed E-commerce to keep up with the pace of the Internet age. With reliable, prompt and professional services, we have established a solid and comprehensive website www.andarocky.com to share Chinese new and good-quality products with best prices and create business opportunities for friends in Africa. We hope that everyone can get what is needed here and become a professional buyer!

Quality first, customer uppermost! Anda Rocky aims in making China and Africa closer and closer!
